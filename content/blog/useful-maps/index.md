After the first [Stronger South Cotswolds](https://www.strongersouthcotswolds.co.uk) event, [Greening Tetbury](https://www.greeningtetbury.org) asked us if we could share any useful links to online maps of local watercourses. Here's what we came up with.

## Water, Sewage, and Floods

- GOV.UK
  - [Check Flood Risk](https://check-long-term-flood-risk.service.gov.uk/map)
  - [Flood Map for Planning](https://flood-map-for-planning.service.gov.uk/map) - shows Flood Zones 2 and 3
- Environment Agency
  - [Statutory main river map](https://environment.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=17cd53dfc524433980cc333726a56386)
  - [Hydrology explorer](https://environment.data.gov.uk/hydrology/landing) - Rainfall, river, and groundwater measurements
  - [Catchment explorer](https://environment.data.gov.uk/catchment-planning)
  - [Water Quality explorer](https://environment.data.gov.uk/water-quality)
  - [Rainfall monitoring stations](https://environment.data.gov.uk/flood-monitoring/assets/demo/index.html)
  - [RPA Land Data](https://environment.data.gov.uk/rpa/) - field/hedge stewardship payments (farmers and land agents only)
- Ordnance Survey
  - [OS Open Rivers](https://www.arcgis.com/home/item.html?id=6048e8a5e29b49b093e79f08c732f109#overview)
- Open Rivers Network
  - [Network map](https://www.openrivers.net/map/) - all rivers as a topologically connected network
  - [Network map with Catchment Partnership data](https://www.arcgis.com/apps/mapviewer/index.html?layers=fd2f3ec65ec54203b99eb04335b160eb)
- Wessex Water
  - [Coast and River watch](https://www.wessexwater.co.uk/coast-and-rivers-watch-map)
  - [Network maps](https://developerservices.wessexwater.co.uk/network-maps) - water mains and sewers
- [Top of the Poops](https://top-of-the-poops.org)
- Rivers Trust
  - [Find your local trust](https://theriverstrust.org/about-us/member-trusts)
  - [Ecological health map](https://theriverstrust.org/ecology-map)

## General purpose maps

- DEFRA
  - [Data services explorer](https://environment.data.gov.uk/explore/9f0fa3fc-a860-4729-adc9-47fe53f658d0)
  - [Multi-Agency Geographic Information for the Countryside (MAGIC) Maps](https://magic.defra.gov.uk)
- [National Library of Scotland](https://maps.nls.uk) - the best source of historical mapping, including:
  - [Ordnance Survey Six-inch to the mile, 1892-1960](https://maps.nls.uk/openlayers/?m=1&id=6)
  - [Ordnance Survey One-inch to the mile, 1906-1917](https://maps.nls.uk/geo/explore/#zoom=7.0&lat=52.92397&lon=-2.33013&layers=272&b=ESRIWorld&o=100)
  - [Ordnance Survey National Grid maps, 1944-1975](https://maps.nls.uk/geo/explore/#zoom=6.0&lat=55.25350&lon=-2.69900&layers=173&b=ESRIWorld&o=100)
- [British Geological Survey (BGS)](https://webapps.bgs.ac.uk/data/MapsPortal/)
  - [Source to Stream](https://sourcetostream.bgs.ac.uk) - catchment explorer
  - [Geology Viewer](https://geologyviewer.bgs.ac.uk/)
- Ordnance Survey
  - [Explore OS Maps](https://explore.osmaps.com)
  - [OS Boundary Line](https://www.arcgis.com/apps/mapviewer/index.html?layers=9dc28220fa9a43cb869af77c0ac484c8) - county/authority boundaries
- Office for National Statistics (ONS)
  - [Map of Parish boundaries](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/datasets/ons::parishes-may-2024-boundaries-ew-bgc-2/explore)
  - [Map of Counties and Unitary Authorities](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/search?q=BDY_CTYUA%202025&sort=Title%7Ctitle%7Casc)
  - [Map of Local Authority districts](https://geoportal.statistics.gov.uk/search?q=BDY_LAD%20DEC_2025&sort=Title%7Ctitle%7Casc)
- [LIDAR Map UK](https://lidarmap.uk) - unofficial map built using Environment Agency data
- [Britain from Above](https://www.britainfromabove.org.uk/en/map?country=global&view=map) - historical photos from WW2
- [UK Met Office maps and charts](https://weather.metoffice.gov.uk/maps-and-charts)

## Local Authority maps

- [Wiltshire Open Data Hub](https://wiltshire-council-open-data-hub-wiltscouncil.hub.arcgis.com/)
- [South Gloucestershire Flood Risk Management](https://bcc.maps.arcgis.com/apps/mapviewer/index.html?webmap=ada238ed59b04d839dc84eceb57db470)

## Other

- [Flood Radar](https://www.floodradar.co.uk) - unofficial aggregator and flood warnings
- [River Levels](https://riverlevels.uk/) - unofficial aggregator and flood warnings
- [Open Infrastructure Map](https://openinframap.org) - power lines and solar farms
- [Historic England Aerial Archaeology](https://historicengland.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html)
- [Natural England Peat Map](https://england-peat-map-portal-ncea.hub.arcgis.com/)
- [Natural England Green Infrastructure](https://designatedsites.naturalengland.org.uk/GreenInfrastructure/Map.aspx) - green spaces
- [UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology](https://catalogue.ceh.ac.uk/explore/spatial/) - land use map
