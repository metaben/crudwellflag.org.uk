One of the first things we did in Crudwell was focus on maintenance, because we felt it had become reactive. We also noticed that there were misunderstandings, even in a small community, about exactly where and what people were talking about.
We created this spreadsheet to itemise what needed doing at all ends of the village.
We're now improving this by creating an annual schedule so that maintenance is done year-in, year-out.

> [!TIP] Having one maintenance list brings transparency and clarity to conversations about what work needs doing, when.

## What software is required?

None. In Crudwell, we are using Google Sheets because:

- we want to share links, not files.
- it's free with a Google Account.
- it is a live editable document, there is only ever one version.
- instead of e-mailing files around all the time, we "publish" the sheet and put the link in a chat channel.
- no software is required, it works across the community on any device.
